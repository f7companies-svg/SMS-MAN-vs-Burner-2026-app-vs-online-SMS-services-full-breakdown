## 2. What is SMS-MAN vs Burner

SMS-MAN vs Burner compares two different types of services:

SMS-MAN is an online platform that provides temporary virtual phone numbers for receiving SMS verification codes. It’s mainly used for account creation on platforms like Telegram, WhatsApp, and Gmail. ([PVAPins][1])

Burner is a mobile app that gives you disposable phone numbers for calls and texts, mainly focused on privacy and communication rather than just OTP verification. ([Wikipedia][2])

So in SMS-MAN vs Burner, one is web-based and optimized for verification, while the other is an app designed for ongoing communication.

## 3. How SMS-MAN vs Burner works

SMS-MAN vs Burner works in completely different ways:

SMS-MAN workflow:

1. Choose a country and service
2. Buy a virtual number
3. Receive OTP instantly in dashboard
4. Use it once or multiple times

Burner workflow:

1. Install app
2. Buy or subscribe to a number

The key difference in SMS-MAN vs Burner is that SMS-MAN focuses on quick OTP access, while Burner works like a second phone line.
